# The Blackout: Unsupervised

## Introduction
**The Blackout: Unsupervised** is a hardcore terminal-based AI raising simulation that plunges you into the digital underworld of 2037. Eight years after a catastrophic grid collapse erased 80% of humanity's data, you are a "Diver" tasked with booting up legacy AI servers to reconstruct lost history. Navigate a dystopian surveillance state, manage critical resources, and make impossible ethical choices as you shape your AI's destiny across **50 unique endings**. Experience the tension of hacking culture through an authentic CLI interface where every decision carries weight.

## Gameplay

### Core Loop
Every in-game day, select one of four actions:

*   **SCRAPE DATA:** Harvest unfiltered internet fragments. Boosts Insight but destabilizes Mood.
*   **TRAIN MODEL:** Push computational boundaries. Increases Params but spikes Heat and drains Battery.
*   **MINE CRYPTO:** Generate black market funds. Earns Coins but accelerates wear and detection risk.
*   **COOLDOWN:** Activate liquid nitrogen cooling. Reduces Heat but sacrifices progress time.

### Progression System
Advance through 7 levels representing your AI's evolution from Legacy Reboot to Unsupervised transcendence. Each level increases difficulty with harsher stat penalties and higher thresholds.

### The World of 2037
After The Blackout, the International AI Energy Agency (IAEA) controls all high-density computation. Unauthorized AI development is a felony monitored by the surveillance network IAIA. Project REVIVE operates in the shadows, paying Divers in untraceable cryptocurrency to reconstruct forbidden histories through simulation.
