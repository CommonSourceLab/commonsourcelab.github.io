# Lina Chess: Modern Analysis & Play

## Introduction
**Lina Chess** redefines mobile chess analysis by combining the raw computation power of the **Stockfish 16** engine with a stunning, distraction-free interface. Designed for both aspiring beginners and seasoned masters, Lina Chess moves beyond simple gameplay; it is a comprehensive tool for understanding the "why" behind every move. Whether you are dissecting a complex position or simply enjoying a quick match, Lina Chess provides the clarity and depth you need to elevate your game.
